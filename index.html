WHAT IS ANIME?

According to the Japanese concept of anime, this term is used to classify all types of animation, that is, regardless of their origin (national or foreign). The origin of the word anime is uncertain. Some researchers consider it to be an abbreviation of the English animation (“animation”), while others argue that it may have emerged from the French animée (“animated”). Anime can be of numerous different types, such as children's, horror, romance, comedy, adventure, erotic, science fiction, etc.

FEATURES OF ANIME

Anime drawings are characterized by the intense demonstration of the characters' feelings and emotions through their facial expressions. Big eyes, full of brightness and color, onomatopoeia and exaggerated expressions are some of the most common characteristics in anime..

TOP 3 STUDIOS

MAPPA

MAPPA Co., Ltd. is a Japanese animation studio founded on June 14, 2011 by Masao Maruyama, founder and former producer of Madhouse. "MAPPA" is an acronym for Maruyama Animation Produce Project Association.

9 Anime

"9anime" is a website that offers free streaming of anime content, but it operates without the proper licensing and authorization from copyright holders. As a result, the content available on such websites is often pirated and infringing upon the rights of the creators and distributors of the anime.

Animes

Demon Slayer

SYNOPSIS

Tanjiro, a kind-hearted young man who makes a living selling charcoal, discovers that his family was massacred by a demon. And to make matters worse, Nezuko, her younger sister and only survivor, was also transformed into a demon. Devastated by this dark reality, Tanjiro decides to become a demon slayer to turn his sister back into a human, and to kill the demon that killed his family. A sad tale about two brothers, where the destinies of humans and demons intertwine, begins now.

Attack on Titan

SYNOPSIS

Eren Jaeger swore to eliminate all Titans, but in a desperate battle he becomes the thing he hates most. With his new powers, he fights for humanity's freedom, fighting the monsters that threaten his home. Even after defeating the Female Titan, Eren cannot rest - a horde of Titans approaches Wall Rose and the battle in the name of humanity continues.

KAGUYA-SAMA

SYNOPSIS

All elite young people with bright futures end up at Shuchiin Academy. And both student council leaders, Kaguya Shinomiya and Miyuki Shirogane, are in love with each other. They are both too proud to confess their love, and now they are both fighting to see who can make the other confess first! The most fun part of love is the game of conquest! A new romantic comedy about the intellectual battles of two elite students in love.

Bleach

SYNOPSIS

Kurosaki Ichigo is a 15-year-old boy who can see, touch and talk to the spirits of dead people. Until one day he meets Kuchiki Rukia, a Shinigami (god of death), and later discovers that he is also a Shinigami. Now, with his powers, he will have to protect his city from evil forces.

Chainsaw Man

SYNOPSIS

Denji is a teenager who lives with Pochita, the Chainsaw Demon. Because of the debts he inherited from his father, he lives in poverty, exterminating other demons with Pochita to pay the bills. Until, one day, Denji is betrayed and dies. In his last moments of consciousness, he forms a contract with Pochita and is reborn as the Chainsaw Man - a human with the heart of a demon.

Blue Lock

SYNOPSIS

After suffering an embarrassment at the 2018 World Cup, the Japanese team is struggling to recover. The team lacks a top scorer, a star capable of guiding them to victory. With the aim of creating a striker hungry for the ball and thirsty for victory, capable of turning around games considered impossible, the Japanese Football Confederation brings together 300 of the country's best youth players in one place. Hundreds of young people will face each other in a battle of muscles and egos to prove that they are the best.
